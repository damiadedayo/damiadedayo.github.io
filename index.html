Robotic Modelling & Drone Skin Design
Dami Adedayo

About This Portfolio

This portfolio documents the development of a prototype solution for robotic applications, from initial concept through to physical production. It covers the virtual-to-physical prototyping journey undertaken during COMP60019 at University of Staffordshire, including an appraisal of the fabrication techniques explored throughout the module.
